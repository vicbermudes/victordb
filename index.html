Mid-thought —

Nuance is novelty.

Nothing is binary.

 

Look into the

dialectical nature

of things.

The Process is Iterative; we are scientific about artistic experimentation.

Leaning In: It’s not that serious, but we take every part of it seriously.

We aim between the lines.

Some shots that have been pulling me

Mirror caution —

Easy to be lost

in one's own

reflection.

 

The mirror is

a check, not

a charm.

Some short films of mine

We aim between the lines.
